"High Proof = Low Grades" Apple Pie

This one will have an extra kick!

  1. Heat up your cider
  2. Add in you cinammon sticks
  3. Add in your brown sugar
  4. Heat to a boil
  5. cool down to less than 170 degrees
  6. 50% ratio of at least 90% Alcohol

"Virgin Cherry" Apple Pie

For all the young'ns

  1. Heat up your cider
  2. Add in you cinammon sticks
  3. Add in your brown sugar
  4. Add in smashed cherries
  5. Heat to a boil
  6. cool down to any temperature you like before consuming
